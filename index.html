about me

helloo, i'm keaton (they/them)! but you may know me as stickertime :) i'm currently a student in singapore and a self-taught writer, artist and coder. i'm only somewhat proficient in html/css, though i'm planning to learn javascript in the near future!

i think i spend waaay too much time online but i'm really interested in web culture. i cycle through many, many hyperfixations, but my other main interests include: psychology, sociology, literature, art, programming and music etc. i just think about the human condition a lot, it's pretty neat!

in my free time, i like playing minecraft, listening to music and reading/writing poetry. if you'd like to hear me ramble about stuff i like, hit me up at my discord @stickertime or check out any of the public social media i have below!
